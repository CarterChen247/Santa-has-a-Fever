Introduction

"Santa has a Fever" is a game based on mobile devices which can teach children to learn the English words of colors and numbers.

Applications as a Learning Material

1.Mobile

The game can be played on mobile devices. It is proven that the colors and the items in the game can attract kids effectively.

2.Kinect

The game can track the motion of an user. By detecting the gestures and position of user's hand and body, it can trigger specific events.

We've been also assessed the effectiveness of the game as a learning material. We had submitted our findings "應用體感數位遊戲式教學於國小中年級英語字彙學習之成效影響(Traditional Chinese)" to "2014 Advanced Management Information Technologies and Applications Conference(AMITAC2014)", and it was included in the proceedings of the conference.
